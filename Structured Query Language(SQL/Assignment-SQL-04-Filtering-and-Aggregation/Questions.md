# SQL Assignment 04 - Filtering and Aggregation

Dataset: Sales Transaction Dataset

## Objective
Practice SQL filtering, logical operators, aggregation, grouping, and sorting.

---

## Question 1
Write a query to find all transactions where the **amount is greater than $1000**.

---

## Question 2

---

## Question 3

---

## Question 4

---

## Question 5

---

## Question 6

---

## Question 7

---

## Question 8

## Question 9
