# Assignment 10 - Probability and Distributions

## Q1
A dataset has a mean of 50 and a standard deviation of 5.  
What percentage of values lie between 45 and 55 if the data follows a normal distribution?

---

## Q2
A group of students has test scores that follow a normal distribution with a mean of 70 and a standard deviation of 10.  
What percentage of students scored above 80?

---

## Q3
Heights of adults in a city follow a normal distribution with a mean of 160 cm and a standard deviation of 8 cm.  
What is the probability that a randomly selected adult is shorter than 150 cm?

---

## Q4
A dataset represents income distribution in a neighborhood, with most individuals earning between $30,000–$50,000, but a few earning over $500,000.  
Is this distribution positively or negatively skewed? Why?

---

## Q5
Retirement ages of employees show that most individuals retire between 60–65 years, with a small number retiring earlier due to health reasons.  
Is this distribution positively or negatively skewed? Explain.

## Q6

The dataset shows frequent extreme values in both tails.  
What type of kurtosis does the distribution exhibit? Why?

## Q7
